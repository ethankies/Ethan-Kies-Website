Simulation of How Viruses Spread

Sick with COVID-19, I grew bored in Virginia Tech's quarantine dorm. I decided to model how the disease was spreading among its then unvaccinated population.

By letting the simulation run, a nice bell curve begins to take shape, demonstrating the exponential behavior of a disease like COVID-19. Click the "Reset Simulation" button then try slowing the spread by making the students social distance. The sooner social distance is implemented, the slower the rate of transmission.
